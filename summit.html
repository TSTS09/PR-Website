Global Context and Purpose

Why Ghana. Why now.

Ghana stands at a pivotal moment. Culturally rich, creatively bold, and rising fast in both the regional and global fashion economy.

The Business of Ghanaian Fashion Summit responds to the urgent need for investment, visibility, and coordination in Ghana's fashion sector. By bringing together players across the value chain, we are catalyzing growth grounded in policy, trade, education, and innovation.

Summit Objectives

The Business of Ghanaian Fashion Summit is a strategic convening built to spark lasting impact across Ghana's fashion value chain.

Strategic Alignment

Align national and international stakeholders through dialogue and shared vision for the industry's future.

Policy Frameworks

Shape policy frameworks that support production, export readiness, and long-term sustainable growth.

Investment Attraction

Introduce investors to high-potential fashion enterprises through data, insights, and visibility.

Global Elevation

Elevate Ghanaian fashion by showcasing excellence, innovation, and cultural depth on global stages.

Cross-Sector Collaboration

Encourage collaboration between creatives, industry experts, and development institutions.

Address Key Challenges

Tackle skills gaps, financing, infrastructure, and institutional support challenges.

This is not just a gathering. It is a launchpad for long-term transformation.

Program Experience

The 2025 Summit at Kempinski Hotel Gold Coast City in Accra offers a one-day experience blending strategic dialogue, brand storytelling, and meaningful networking.

Keynote Speakers

Insightful voices leading the conversation on creative economies, policy, trade, and innovation from local and international thought leaders.

Panel Discussions

Engaging conversations led by stakeholders from government, education, fashion, finance, development organizations, and institutional partners.

Breakout Sessions & Roundtables

Smaller forums for focused engagement on specific challenges and solutions, fostering deeper collaboration and problem-solving.

Pop-Up Exhibitions & Brand Showcases

Immersive installations celebrating Ghanaian creativity and craftsmanship, providing visibility for local designers and brands.

Networking Cocktail

An evening reception for connection, partnership building, and celebration of Ghana's fashion community and international collaborators.
